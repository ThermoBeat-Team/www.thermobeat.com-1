What is ThermoBeat?

ThermoBeat uses thermal energy to charge pacemakers.

Currently, the majority of patients with pacemakers recquire surgery every 5-7 years. This places a great deal of medical, financial and emotional stresses on patients. ThermoBeat harnesses the body's thermal energy, as opposed to batteries, to maintain its functionality. This reduces the number of surgeries recquired by patients and provides a plethora of benefits.

Medical

Problem: Repitive pacemaker surgery requires physical surgery, which strains the body, especially as patients get older.
Solution: As ThermoBeats recquires no surgeries to replace batteries, less stress is put on the body of patients, reducing physical stress and recovery time. The app will alert medical professionals if patient experiences medical emergency (heart attack ...). If there is a malfunction of the pacemaker, there is a backup lithium iodine battery installed as a precaution.

Emotional

Problem: Repititive surgery causes patient to feel financial and emotional burden on families. May also create emotional stress for families of patients, especially those who are older.
Solution: Families worry less about the patient, who now doesnâ€™t need as many surgeries. As well, an app will be developed in the future to allow patients and family members to check their data to ensure their health, reducing emotional stress.

Financial

Problem: Pacemaker replacement is a very expensive procedure, and usually costs upwards of $10,000 each time. This puts a burden on patients and their families, as they have to save for this inevitable cost every few years. The patient cannot work, family members may not be able to work due to care for patient.
Solution: Fewer surgeries results in less financial costs for patients, reducing financial burden. As well, patients will not miss work due to surgery and family members will not be recquired to care for patients during surgery and rehabilitation.
